## Overview

The CodexPad-C10 is a low-power Bluetooth gamepad within the CodexPad series, specifically designed for makers and embedded developers. The series features an innovative Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) master-slave integrated design, offering high connectivity flexibility. As one of the products in this series, the CodexPad-C10 not only supports operating as a peripheral to connect with various devices possessing BLE central capabilities but its series design also enables it to function as a central device to interact with other BLE peripherals.

The CodexPad series is broadly compatible with mainstream embedded development boards, including the **micro:bit**, **ESP32**/**ESP32-S**/**ESP32-C** series, providing a reliable physical input solution for projects such as robot control, IoT device interaction, and custom human-machine interfaces. Inheriting the core advantages of the series, the CodexPad-C10 provides simple and easy-to-use data interfaces, allowing developers to effortlessly read button states and joystick analog values.

We are committed to delivering a developer-friendly experience for the maker ecosystem. We not only provide extensive host-side libraries and sample code but also support graphical programming environments, enabling everyone from beginners to professional developers to get started quickly and turn their ideas into reality.
